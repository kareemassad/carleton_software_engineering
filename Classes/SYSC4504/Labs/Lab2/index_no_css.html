History

Microsoft Corporation created Xbox, a video game console system. Released in 2001, Xbox was Microsoft's first entry into the world of console electronic gaming, which placed it in direct competition with Sony's PlayStation 2 and Nintendo's GameCube.

Versions of the consoles available

  • Xbox, 2001
  • Xbox 360, 2005
  • Xbox One, 2023

    • Xbox One X, 2017
  • Xbox Series X, 2020
